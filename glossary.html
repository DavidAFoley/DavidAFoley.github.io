Glossary

Aerial photography has a lot of terminology and abbreviations that make it hard to understand. I try not to use them, but where I can't avoid using them, I'll try and list each here with a definition to make it easier to understand.


Term Definition More information
CAA Civil Aviation Authority - the organisation responsible for the UK airspace, including drones CAA
DJI A large manufacturer of consumer and professional drones DJI
FPV First Person View - transmitting the view from the drone's camera to the screen of your device (e.g. phone), remote controller or goggles, so that you can see what is happening from the perspective of the drone.
Gimbal The mechanism that keeps the camera stable (and thus able to take useful video) whilst in flight.
LiPo Lithium Polymer - the most commonly used battery type in drones (and in mobile phones and laptops).
NATS The National Air Traffic Services - responsible for air traffic control in the UK NATS
PfCO Permission for Commercial Operation - which can be obtained from the CAA and is required before any work can be undertaken for which you will be paid or receive some benefit in return for it.
Quad Quadcopter/Quadrocopter - a drone with four vertical propellers
VLOS Visual Line Of Sight. You must be able to see your drone when it is flying. Normally thought to be no more than 500m and often considerably less depending on conditions. CAA definition
