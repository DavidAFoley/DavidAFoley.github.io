About Me

I started flying aerial cameras a couple of years ago after thinking about them for quite a while. I fell in love with the experience straight away and have not looked back since.

You'll find plenty of material here about aerial photography. It's something I love doing, but recognise that it's a relatively new field and one that generates contrasting emotions and views from people. As a result, it's worth talking about.
